Rooms

Year Published:

2021

Rooms is a personal project with no real message, just a challenge for me to overcome. In march, my goal was to make a project using skills I had just learned and see what I could make within two weeks. I wanted to test myself and find a way to express my love for snappy animation and colourful designs, as well as compositing too. Ironically this project started out as a mistake (an idea pushed by the hype of a trend: NFTS) that ended up turning into something great. I'm glad to finally be able to the share the process of making it.

Character Animation

Since the theme was based off of Retro games. I wanted these faces to add immersion, as if the viewer would run into end game bosses. My goal was to make these animations playful and expressive. And I believe they turned out great.

CRT

With this being the original idea and sole focus of Rooms. Putting together this CRT effect was definitely a fun learning experience. Two different variants were scrapped before this result. Implementing this into the project helped not only with immersion and detail, But a clearer direction of where I wanted to take this entirely. Definitely will be exploring this more in future projects.
