Over the Summer I was commissioned to make a Valorant themed intro for Synergy (a gaming org). With my appreciation for both Worship Studio & Valorant itself. I took the opportunity to try re-engineering valorant's branding animations. This was a very fun and extremely challenging project; as I was aiming to make it as accurate as possible. Very glad to be able to share this here.
